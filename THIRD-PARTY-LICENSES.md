# Third-Party Licenses

the open-source components listed below. Their copyright notices and license
texts are reproduced here in compliance with their terms.

This file is auto-generated from the crate dependency graph by `cargo about`
(`make licenses`). Two additional components are **not** Rust crates and are
documented manually at the end: the vendored **whisper.cpp** C/C++ sources and
the **OpenCC** conversion dictionaries. The speech models downloaded at install
